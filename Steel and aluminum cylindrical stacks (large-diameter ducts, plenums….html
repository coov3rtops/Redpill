Recommended Sizes

Retain the proven large scale from Falcon 9 first-stage tanks for excellent airflow capacity while simplifying for static data-center use (no cryogenics, flight loads, or pressurization needed).

  • Length per module/stack: 10–20 m practical modules (easier transport, installation, and modularity). Full ≈40–42 m lengths are feasible for local Ohio fabrication and vertical exhaust stacks.

  • Features for cooling use: Internal baffles/vanes for directed airflow, flanges or modular connections, access ports, mounting points for fans/filters/sensors, coatings for corrosion resistance, and optional fins or insulation.
